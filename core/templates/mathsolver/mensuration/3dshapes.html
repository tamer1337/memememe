Geometric Shapes Properties

Shape Volume Lateral Surface Area Total Surface Area
Cube V = a³ LSA = 4a² TSA = 6a²
Cuboid V = l × w × h LSA = 2h(l + w) TSA = 2(lw + lh + wh)
Sphere V = (4/3)πr³ LSA = 4πr² TSA = 4πr²
Hemisphere V = (2/3)πr³ LSA = 2πr² TSA = 3πr²
Cylinder V = πr²h LSA = 2πrh TSA = 2πr(r + h)
Cone V = (1/3)πr²h LSA = πrl TSA = πr(r + l)
Frustum V = (1/3)πh(R² + Rr + r²) LSA = πl(R + r) TSA = π[R² + r² + l(R + r)]

Legend:

  • a = side length (cube)
  • l, w, h = length, width, height (cuboid)
  • r = radius
  • h = height
  • l = slant height (cone/frustum)
  • R, r = top and bottom radii (frustum)
